# Mood Application
Use high contrast, vivid saturated accent colors against deep dark background, heavy visual weight with dramatic energy. The central visual element should feel dynamic and urgent, with sharp transitions and bold color pops. Dark background (80% area) with electric accent colors creating tension and forward momentum.

# Font Application
Use clean geometric sans-serif typography for both title and subtitle. Title should be bold and large (20-25% of image height), subtitle smaller and lighter weight below. White or near-white text against dark background. Modern, authoritative, professional letterforms.

# Composition
Type composition (conceptual):
- Abstract geometric shapes representing core concepts: energy, data, time, transformation
- Clean information zones: visual area (65%), text area (35%, bottom or right)
- Layered depth: background grid → mid-layer data patterns → foreground energy wave
- Visual hierarchy: energy curve is the dominant element, factory elements secondary

Visual composition:
- Main visual: A dramatic glowing electricity price waveform/curve sweeping horizontally across the composition, rendered as a sharp polyline with bright electric cyan and amber nodes at peaks and troughs. The waveform transitions from orderly (left, representing old fixed pricing) to chaotic and dynamic (right, representing AI-driven real-time adaptation). Behind the curve, subtle factory silhouettes and industrial grid patterns in negative space. Above the curve, abstract data nodes and AI network patterns suggesting intelligent optimization.
- Layout: 4:3 aspect ratio. Visual occupies top 55% with the energy wave as the hero element, surrounded by data visualization elements. Title "40年电价退场" positioned in the center-bottom zone in bold white sans-serif. Subtitle "制造业真正的淘汰赛，开始了" in a lighter weight below the title. A thin electric cyan accent line separates text zone from visual zone. More vertical space allows larger title and stronger visual impact on mobile.
- Decorative: Subtle geometric grid lines (like a power grid or circuit board) in the negative space. Small data node dots at key curve inflection points. Faint industrial gear/cog motifs in the deep background. A subtle glow/bloom effect around the energy waveform.

Color scheme:
- Primary background: Deep navy black (#0D1117) to steel gray (#1A1F2E) gradient
- Accent 1: Electric cyan (#00D4FF) — the energy waveform, key highlights
- Accent 2: Amber/Warning (#FF8C00) — data nodes, price peaks, urgency signals
- Accent 3: Cool steel (#4A5568) — grid lines, factory silhouettes, secondary elements
- Text: Near-white (#F0F0F5) for title, slightly muted (#C8CCD4) for subtitle

Rendering notes (digital):
- Sharp, precise lines with clean edges — no sketchy or hand-drawn quality
- Smooth gradients and glows for the energy waveform
- Crisp geometric grid patterns
- Polished, professional finish — like a high-end tech product visualization
- Subtle grain or noise texture in the dark background to add depth without softening the precision

Type notes (conceptual):

- Information-rich but not cluttered — every element has a purpose
- Visual metaphor: the collision between old fixed systems and new dynamic intelligence
- Left-to-right narrative: past (orderly, fixed) → present (disruption, chaos) → future (AI-optimized, dynamic)

Palette notes (cool):
