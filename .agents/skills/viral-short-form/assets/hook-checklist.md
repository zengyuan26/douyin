# Hook checklist

Run any candidate hook through this. A strong hook clears most of these; if it
fails the first three, rewrite it before going further.

- [ ] **Lands in ~2 seconds** when said out loud. No setup, no "hey guys."
- [ ] **Leads with payoff or stake**, not context or brand.
- [ ] **Concrete**: has a specific noun, number, or named stake.
- [ ] **Opens a loop or states a consequence** the viewer needs resolved.
- [ ] **Reads as a hook on mute** (works as on-screen text alone).
- [ ] **Promise is one the video can actually pay off** (no orphan clickbait).
- [ ] **Targeted enough** that the right viewer feels "this is for me."
- [ ] **Distinct archetype** from the other options offered (diversity, not
      variations).

Fast upgrades when a hook is weak:
- Add a number or a dollar/time figure.
- Name the exact viewer ("if you [situation]…").
- Cut everything before the first interesting word.
- Swap a vague benefit for a concrete result.
- Turn a statement into a withheld reveal ("I won't say the brand, but…").
